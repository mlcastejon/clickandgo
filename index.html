Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry

Established around the 10th century, Hogwarts is considered to be one of the finest magical institutions in the wizarding world, though other notable schools included Beauxbatons Academy of Magic and the Durmstrang Institute. This year, Hogwarts is launching for the very first time a Introduction to Witchcraft Summercamp for foreign students. It consists in 4 weeks where the students could learn from the best warlocks and wizards.
